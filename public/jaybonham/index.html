03The Method

The Precision
Loop.

Jay's five-protocol system. Run it in order, every session — then the loop closes and the last step feeds the next trade. Not theory: a standing operating procedure for traders.

01 Strategy· 02 Risk· 03 Psychology· 04 Execution· 05 Review↻
01
Protocol 01 · Strategy

The Setup Filter

Only setups you can name and defend make the cut. Defined entries, a hard invalidation level, and zero improvising at the open.

OutputIf you can't name it, you don't take it.
02
Protocol 02 · Risk

The Risk Envelope

Size and stop are fixed before entry — a set percent per trade and a capped daily loss. A bad day stays a bad day, never a blown account.

OutputRisk is set before the open.
03
Protocol 03 · Psychology

The Cold Mind

A pre-trade state you can repeat. Breathe, check the plan, kill the revenge trade — train the mind like an athlete so emotion stops trading for you.

OutputSame trader, green or red.
04
Protocol 04 · Execution

The Trigger Routine

A checklist that fires the same way every time. Hesitation and impulse both removed — you stop deciding in the moment and simply run the routine.

OutputOne routine. Every entry.
05
Protocol 05 · Review → loop

The Honest Review

Journal every trade and grade the decision, not just the result. Lessons feed straight back into Protocol 01 — and the loop compounds your edge.

OutputThe loop closes. Edge compounds.
